How we started

2020 was a year of solitude and self-reflection for most with lockdown and Covid fears running rampant in New York City. For fear of getting others sick or getting sick yourself, people isolated from each other and spent time realigning their priorities. Like most, many of our friends craved human interaction after being starved of the opportunity to cook a meal together, open a bottle of wine for the table, or sit down and enjoy each other’s company.

2021 was the time for reemergence – that was the beginning of Sunday Dinner.

Just a couple of blokes enjoying each other's company...

Justin Law

An Australian expatriate trying to build a sense of family around him, Justin, affectionately known as JLaw, spends every Sunday dinner with his closest friends.

Justin is currently based in NYC working as a technology strategist for McKinsey. He is often found reading psychology books to better understand his concussion-shaken mind in El Condor Cafe located in the West Village.

Outside of his professional and intellectual pursuits, Justin enjoys playing Water Polo, lock-picking, snowboarding, running, climbing and surfing.
