How we started

Just a couple of blokes who chilled out consistently and realized the pattern...

Justin Law

An Australian expatriate trying to build a sense of family around him, Justin, affectionately known as JLaw, started spending every Sunday cooking for his friends in hopes that they would join him on a weekly basis.

Justin is currently based in NYC, working as a technology strategist for McKinsey. He is often found reading psychology books to better understand his concussion-shaken mind in El Condor Cafe located in West Village, Manhattan.

Outside of his professional and intellectual pursuits, Justin enjoys playing Water Polo, lock-picking, snowboarding, running, climbing and surfing.
